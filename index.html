About

It was 1996 AC Earth Bound Time, when it all started!

It was a diffrent time Back then, there were no starships, No inter glactic travel and No way to be a baller. Computers changed everything!

Personal Computers were fascinating, even when they came out. Good old clunky mouce clicks could do more math than I could do in my entire life

In 2003 EBT, I was 9 and my dad started teaching me about this thing called Internet. Now, with one click you could connect to anyone one! ANYONE

I was SOLD

After a Decade Later I started working with those machines. I could code them. I used stuff like React, redux, Node, mongo, Express to create products on the intenet that could tell stories. my stories.
